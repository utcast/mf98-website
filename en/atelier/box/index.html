Light Box

Materials

If you come to make the light box, please check if the materials listed here are available.

Make a tube with construction paper

Fold along the dotted line so that the squares of the construction paper are visible. Finally, peel off the double-sided tape and stick it together.

Fold the tracing paper along the line and cover the tube

Fold the tracing paper along the line and cover the tube.

Roll the silver paper with the silver side facing in

Try making various shapes such as circles, triangles, and stars!

Fill the box with silver paper

Cover with colored cellophane

Cover the entire surface with 4 sheets of colored cellophane. If you layer the colored cellophane, you can create interesting colors by mixing them!

Finish

The Light Box is a craft designed by Taizō Matsumura.
