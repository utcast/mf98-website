Clothes Booth

Difference in weaving method

In this booth, we have prepared models of plain weave, satin weave, and twill weave made with tape and string. When you have time, we will introduce the differences in functionality due to each weaving method. We will also explain the characteristics of each weaving method while introducing actual usage examples, as the fabric has different strength and elasticity depending on the weaving method. This way, you can learn how the differences in weaving methods are related to daily life.

UV beads experiment

In this experiment, we will look at the differences in the properties of clothing due to color. Clothing has the effect of blocking ultraviolet rays, but the degree of this effect varies depending on the color of the clothing. When ultraviolet rays hit, the color changes beads (UV beads) are placed on top of clothing with different colors, and we will see how the color of UV beads changes when exposed to ultraviolet rays.

Water absorption and quick-drying experiment

In this experiment, we will look at the differences in how clothing materials absorb water. First, we will compare how cotton and polyester fabrics absorb water and explain the reasons for the differences. Then, we will conduct an experiment to see which material dries faster and explain the reasons based on the principles discussed in the water absorption experiment.
